    What is Assassins Creed Unity

    Assassin's Creed Unity is an action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and nine other Ubisoft development studios. The game was published by Ubisoft and released in Europe on November 13, 2014. It is the seventeenth installment in the Assassin's Creed series and the sequel to Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag, which came out in 2013.

    What is the storyline of the game?

    The main story is set in Paris during the French Revolution from 1789 to 1794, and follows Assassin Arno Dorian and his efforts to expose the true powers behind the Revolution, while seeking revenge against those responsible for his adoptive father's murder. ... Assassin's Creed Unity received mixed reviews upon release.
