    What is Assassins Brotherhood

    Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood is an action-adventure computer game developed by Ubisoft for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. The European release date for this game was November 19, 2010 for consoles, and March 18, 2011 for PC in Europe. The game is a direct sequel to Assassin's Creed II and the story is set right after the end of Assassin's Creed II, with Ezio Auditore da Firenze once again as the protagonist. Unlike the previous two parts, this game does feature multiplayer. On November 15, 2016, the game was released along with Assassin's Creed II and Assassin's Creed Revelations on the PlayStation 4 & Xbox One as The Ezio Collection

    What is the storyline of the game?

    The main story spans the years 1500-1507 and continues from the events of Assassin's Creed II, as Ezio takes the fight against the Templars (led by the influential Borgia family) to Rome, where he attempts to rebuild the Assassin Brotherhood in Italy and liberate the city from the Borgias' control.
