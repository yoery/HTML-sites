    What is Assassins Creed III

    Assassin's Creed III is the fifth computer game in the Assassin's Creed series, developed and published by Ubisoft. The game was released on October 30, 2012 in the United States and on October 31 in Europe. Compared to the previous installments, the game features a new protagonist, Connor Kenway, and a different era, that of the American War of Independence
