    What is Assassins Creed IV Black Flag

    Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag is a computer game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. It is the thirteenth installment in the Assassin's Creed series and the sequel to 2012's Assassin's Creed III. Worldwide, the game was released on October 29, 2013 for PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. The versions for PlayStation 4, PC and Wii U were released in the Netherlands and Belgium on November 21. The release date for the Xbox One version was on November 22. The game had shipped 10 million times worldwide before the end of 2013. On December 6, 2019, the game was released along with Assassin's Creed Rogue on the Nintendo Switch as The Rebel Collection.
