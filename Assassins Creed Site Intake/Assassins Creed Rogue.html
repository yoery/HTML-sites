    What is Assassins Creed Rogue

    Assassin's Creed Rogue is an action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft development studios in Sofia, Bucharest, Chengdu, Milan, Montreal, Quebec and Singapore. Published by Ubisoft, the game was released in Europe on November 13, 2014 for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. On March 10, 2015, the game was also released for Windows. The game is part of the Assassin's Creed series, but unlike all console games in the series since 2010, Rogue does not have a multiplayer mode; only a single player.

    What is the storyline of the game?

    The main plot is set before and during the French and Indian War from 1752 to 1760, and follows Shay Patrick Cormac, an Irish American Assassin-turned-Templar who hunts down former members of his Brotherhood after becoming disillusioned with their tactics.
