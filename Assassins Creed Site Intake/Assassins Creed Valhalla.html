    What is Assassins Valhalla

    Assassin's Creed Valhalla is an action-adventure game in developed by Ubisoft Montreal. The game was released on November 10, 2020 by Ubisoft for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Xbox Series X, and Stadia. On November 12, 2020, the game also became playable on PlayStation 5. The game is set in 873 AD and tells a fictional story about the Viking invasion of Britain. The player controls Eivor, a Viking raider who becomes embroiled in the conflict between the ''Brotherhood of Assassins'' and the Templar Order.

    What is the storyline of the game?

    Assassin's Creed Valhalla is an open-world action-adventure game built around several main storylines and optional side missions called "World Events." The player takes on the role of Eivor Varinsdottir, a Viking raider, as they lead their fellow Vikings against the Anglo-Saxon kingdoms.
