    What is Assassins Creed Origins

    Assassin's Creed Origins is an action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft Montreal. The game was released on October 27, 2017 by Ubisoft for PlayStation 4, Windows, and Xbox One. It is the tenth major game in the Assassin's Creed series and the successor to 2015's Assassin's Creed Syndicate.

    What is the storyline of the game?

    Principally set in Egypt, near the end of the Ptolemaic period (49–43 BC), the story follows a Medjay named Bayek of Siwa and his wife Aya, and explores the origins of the millennia-long conflict between the Hidden Ones—forerunners to the Assassin Brotherhood—who fight for peace by promoting liberty, and The Order of the Abcients
